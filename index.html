About

I build things, mostly at the edge of software and hardware. Grew up in Rewa, a small city in Madhya Pradesh. Dropped out of college to travel across India — spent a year figuring things out on the road before coming back to build.

Currently working on a device to restore speech for people who lose their voice after stroke or surgery. Before that, shipped two products from scratch with no funding. I like problems that seem solved but aren't.

Building Now

Resonance active

A wearable device for speech restoration. Millions of people lose the ability to speak after stroke or throat surgery — existing devices sound robotic because they vibrate the neck tissue, which mostly turns into heat. Working on a different approach: controlled air-pressure patterns shaped by ML, producing sound through the mouth cavity instead. Early stage, clinical partners in place.
